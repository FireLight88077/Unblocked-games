General Gameplay

Geometry Dash Lite challenges players to guide a geometric icon through obstacle-filled levels synced to music. Your icon can jump or fly, but if it collides with any obstacle, you have to replay the level. You will experience thrilling levels filled with moving platforms, spikes, lasers, etc.! How to Controls

You can use one of three keys here to make the character jump:

  • Spacebar
  • Up arrow key
  • Left mouse button

If you play in practice mode, you can press the Z key to activate checkpoints and use the X key to remove the closest one.
